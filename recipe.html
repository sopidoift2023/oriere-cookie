Chocolate Cookie Recipe

Ingredients

  • and 1/4 cups (280g) all-purpose flour (spooned & leveled)
    • 2
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons cornstarch*
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup (170g) unsalted butter, melted & cooled 5 minutes*
  • 3/4 cup (150g) packed light or dark brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup (100g) granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg + 1 egg yolk, at room temperature
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 1 and 1/4 cups (225g) semi-sweet chocolate chips or chocolate chunks

Baking instructions

Follow this steps to make an Amazing chocolate chip cookie.

  1. Preheat the oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Prepare dry ingredients: In a medium bowl, combine the flour, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
  3. Mix butter and sugars: In a large mixing bowl, beat the butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar together until creamy and smooth.
  4. Add eggs and vanilla: Beat in the eggs one at a time, followed by the vanilla extract, until the mixture is smooth and well combined.
  5. Combine dry ingredients: Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Avoid overmixing.
  6. Add chocolate chips: Fold in the chocolate chips using a spatula or spoon.
  7. Prepare cookie sheets: Line cookie sheets with parchment paper or lightly grease them.
  8. Scoop dough: Using a spoon or cookie scoop, drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto the prepared cookie sheets, spacing them about 2 inches apart.
  9. Bake cookies: Bake the cookies in the preheated oven for 8 to 10 minutes or until the edges are lightly golden.
  10. Cool cookies: Remove the cookies from the oven and let them cool on the cookie sheets for a few minutes. Then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.
  11. Enjoy: Once the cookies have cooled, enjoy your freshly baked chocolate chip cookies!

    12. These instructions should give your chocolate cookie a very good look and taste!
